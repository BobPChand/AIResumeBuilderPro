AI Resume Builder Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Resume Builder Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

AI Resume Builder Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers beyond what is needed to generate your resume or cover letter. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using device storage and is never transmitted to our servers for storage:

  • Your profile: Name, contact details, work experience, education, and skills you enter are stored locally on your device.
  • Generated content: Resumes and cover letters you generate are saved locally in your history.
  • Subscription status: Your subscription status is stored locally to determine app features.

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data You Provide for Content Generation

When you generate a resume or cover letter, your profile information and the job description you paste in are sent to our AI backend for processing. This data is used solely to generate your requested content and is not stored on our servers after the response is returned.

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, our third-party payment provider. We do not receive or store your credit card information. Stripe handles all payment data in accordance with PCI compliance standards. For more information, see Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Used for AI resume and cover letter generation. Your profile data and job description are sent to OpenAI for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Used for subscription payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

AI Resume Builder Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
